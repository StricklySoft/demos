How we work

A Security Program Built Around You

No cookie-cutter contracts. We assess your real risks, build a plan that fits your site and budget, and stay accountable with reporting you can actually see.

Assess

A licensed supervisor walks your property, identifies vulnerabilities, and reviews your goals — at no cost or obligation.

Plan

We design a tailored coverage plan: post orders, patrol schedules, escalation procedures, and transparent pricing.

Deploy

Vetted, trained, and uniformed officers are matched to your site and briefed on your specific protocols before day one.

Report

GPS-tracked patrols, time-stamped logs, and a dedicated account manager keep you informed and in control 24/7.
