FAQ

Good questions, answered

How much does it cost to rent a restroom trailer?

It depends on the trailer size, how long you need it, and your location. Daily, weekend, weekly, and monthly rates bring the daily cost down. Send us your details through the Request to Rent form and we'll get you an accurate quote.

Do you offer emergency or same-day service?

Yes — we offer 24/7 emergency service. If a unit goes down or you have an urgent need, call (336) 259-4049 or use the Emergency tab on our request form and we'll respond as fast as possible.

Do the trailers need water and power?

Our trailers are self-contained with onboard fresh-water tanks, but for longer rentals a standard hookup keeps things running smoothly. We'll walk you through exactly what your site needs when we plan the delivery.

How does weekly servicing work?

For ongoing rentals, our team comes out on a set schedule to pump, clean, sanitize, and restock supplies — so the unit stays fresh the entire time it's on your site.

How far in advance should I book?

The sooner the better, especially for wedding and event season. Reach out as early as you can and we'll hold your dates.

What size trailer do I need?

It depends on your guest count and how long the event runs. Not sure? Tell us about your event on the form or give us a call and we'll recommend the right unit — from our 2-station trailers up to the 10 Station Silver.
